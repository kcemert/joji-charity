How We Work

Our Mission in Detail

We exist for one purpose: to ensure that no child in Telangana is denied an education simply because their family cannot pay for it.

🔍

We Verify Every Child

Fr. Joji personally visits each family to confirm genuine financial need. No child is added to the program without this verification. No exceptions.

💯

Zero Admin Costs

There are no salaried staff, no office, and no overhead. Fr. Joji manages everything personally. 100% of every donation reaches a child.

💰

$40 / Month Per Child

This single monthly donation covers a child's full boarding school costs: accommodations, three daily meals, all textbooks, and school supplies.

⛪

95% Catholic Schools

The Warangal Diocese's Catholic boarding schools are known for quality education and values-based formation. Most of our children attend these schools.

👥

Boarding School Access

Many children live in remote villages without nearby quality schools. Boarding school is the only viable path — and your donation makes it possible.

Context

Why Telangana? Why Now?

Telangana is one of India's newer states, carved out of Andhra Pradesh in 2014. Despite significant urban growth in its capital, Hyderabad, rural areas — especially tribal regions — continue to face deep poverty and limited educational access.

In villages surrounding Mariapuram, many families earn less than $2 a day through agricultural labor. The cost of boarding school — which includes meals, books, and supervision — is simply beyond reach without outside support.
