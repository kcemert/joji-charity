Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Does 100% really go to the children?

Yes. Fr. Joji personally manages all operations and receives no compensation. There are no paid staff, no office rent, and no overhead. Every dollar is sent directly to boarding schools to cover a child's costs.

How are children selected?

Fr. Joji visits families personally before accepting any child into the program. He verifies financial need, assesses the child's desire to learn, and ensures there is no other means for the family to afford education.

Can I sponsor a specific child?

At this time, donations support the children collectively. Fr. Joji allocates funds based on each child's needs. We hope to offer direct child-to-sponsor matching as the program grows.

Can I cancel at any time?

Absolutely. Monthly donations can be cancelled at any time through your Stripe account or by contacting us. We never lock donors in — your support is always voluntary.
