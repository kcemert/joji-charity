Our Mission

Transforming Lives Through Education

In the villages surrounding Mariapuram, Telangana, many children come from families who cannot afford the cost of boarding school — even when those schools provide the only path to a better future.

We identify the most vulnerable children, verify their need, and ensure every dollar sponsors exactly what it promises: a safe place to sleep, nutritious meals, textbooks, and school supplies.

"Education is the most powerful tool we can give these children. With your support, we make sure no child is left behind for want of $40 a month."
— Fr. Joji Reddy Thirumalareddy
